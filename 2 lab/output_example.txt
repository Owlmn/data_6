Title: Income tax collection rose 16 pc in five months Dar
Published: 12/28/2015

Summary:
Pakistan's Finance Minister Ishaq Dar stated that the country has made significant economic progress since 2013, citing commendations from international institutions. He highlighted the government's focus on economy, education, energy, and anti-terrorism, noting a 16 percent rise in income tax collection and the implementation of austerity measures.

Title: Serena swats Roland Garros quarter
Published: 6/1/2016

Summary:
Serena Williams advanced to the French Open quarter-finals with a dominant 6-1, 6-1 victory over Elina Svitolina. The match, delayed by two days due to rain, was played under damp conditions on a sparsely attended centre court. Williams will next face either Carla Suarez Navarro or Yulia Putintseva for a place in the semi-finals.

Title: PM announces Rs5 per litre cut in POL pri
Published: 1/30/2016

Summary:
Prime Minister Nawaz Sharif announced a reduction of Rs5 per litre in petroleum product prices effective February 1. The cut applies to petrol, diesel, and kerosene. The announcement was made while the PM also discussed the National Action Plan and Pakistan's diplomatic efforts between Iran and Saudi Arabia.

Title: no change in petroleum prices next month dar
Published: 4/30/2015

Summary:
Pakistan's Finance Minister Ishaq Dar announced that petroleum prices will remain unchanged for May, rejecting a summary from the Oil and Gas Regulatory Authority (OGRA). OGRA had recommended a slight cut in petrol and diesel prices but a significant increase for HOBC and kerosene oil. The government decided to maintain current prices despite the proposed adjustments.

Title: nec approves rs 1.514 trillion for dev
Published: 6/1/2015

Summary:
Pakistan's National Economic Council has approved a development budget of 1.514 trillion rupees and set a GDP growth target of 5.5 percent for the next financial year. The plan allocates 700 billion rupees for federal projects and 814 billion for provincial programs, with specific growth targets for agriculture and manufacturing. An export goal of 25.5 billion dollars was also established for the year.
