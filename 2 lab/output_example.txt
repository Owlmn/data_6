Title: WC T20 Waqar confident Pakistan will defeat Indi
Published: 3/18/2016

Summary:
Pakistan cricket team head coach Waqar Younis has vowed to defeat India in their upcoming match on March 19 in Kolkata. He expressed confidence, stating that India is under immense pressure and Pakistan will change the course of history. Waqar also highlighted Pakistan's good batting form and superior pace attack compared to India.

Title: imf lowers 2015 global growth forecast to 3.3%
Published: 7/9/2015

Summary:
The IMF lowered its 2015 global economic growth forecast to 3.3% from 3.5%, citing a temporary setback in the US due to severe weather and risks from the Greek debt crisis and China's slowdown. Forecasts for the eurozone and China remained unchanged, but the US, Canada, and Mexico saw downgrades. The IMF noted that advanced economies are recovering while emerging markets slow, with downside risks from geopolitical tensions, and projected 2016 growth at 3.8%, though the pickup has not yet materialized.

Title: Only three Pakistanis ICC top 10 Ranking
Published: 5/31/2016

Summary:
Only three Pakistanis are featured in the latest ICC Player Rankings, with Younis Khan ranked 5th and Misbah ul Haq 10th in Test batting, and Yasir Shah 4th in Test bowling. England's James Anderson has topped the Test Bowlers rankings for the first time, while Moeen Ali achieved a career-high 35th in batting. The rankings also highlight Steve Smith as the top Test batsman and Anderson as the leading bowler.
