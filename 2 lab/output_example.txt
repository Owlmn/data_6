Title: Pakistans first DTH licences auctioned for Rs 1469 billi
Published: 11/24/2016

Summary:
Pakistan's PEMRA auctioned three Direct to Home (DTH) service licenses for a total of Rs 14.69 billion after 15 hours of bidding, with Mag Entertainment placing the highest bid. The DTH service is expected to attract significant investment, create 15,000 jobs, and reduce reliance on illegal Indian DTH services. Licenses are non-exclusive for 15 years, and companies must start operations within a year to avoid termination.

Title: Iran says will resist curbs on oil output as part of global
Published: 2/17/2016

Summary:
Iran has rejected plans to join an OPEC-led oil production freeze pact, insisting on increasing output to pre-sanctions levels. OPEC ministers from Iraq, Qatar, and Venezuela met with Iran to discuss the deal, but Iran remains opposed, arguing it shouldn't bear the cost of market stabilization. The pact, agreed by Saudi Arabia and Russia, aims to address oversupply and low oil prices, but Iran's refusal is a key hurdle.

Title: N Ireland enjoy Euro 2016 win over Ukrai
Published: 6/17/2016

Summary:
Northern Ireland defeated Ukraine 2-0 in Euro 2016, with goals from Gareth McAuley and Niall McGinn, keeping their knockout stage hopes alive after an initial loss to Poland. McAuley became the second oldest goalscorer in European Championship history, and the win was Northern Ireland's first at the finals. Ukraine's chances of advancing are now slim, requiring a win and other results to progress.
